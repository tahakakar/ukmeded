About the Conference

The field of surgery has been revolutionised by the introduction of new technologies and surgical techniques. A major breakthrough is the use of robotic systems like the MAKO system for knee replacements (robotic arm-assisted knee surgery). Consequently, MIS has emerged as the gold standard of care in numerous clinical specialties globally. We are pleased to announce the MarketsandMarkets London Orthopaedic Meeting, scheduled to take place on 13th-14th June 2024, in London, UK. This conference aims to provide a platform for surgeons, physicians, residents, clinical specialty nurses, and other medical professionals to gather and discuss the latest technological advancements, emerging trends, challenges, and future prospects in minimally invasive surgery. The focus will be on managing disorders related to Orthopaedics, ‘also with focus towards the FRCS’. Over the course of two days, attendees will have the opportunity to engage in expert keynote presentations and participate in breakthrough sessions. This scientific meeting will not only facilitate knowledge exchange but also enable networking among peers, ensuring participants stay informed about the most recent developments in the world of minimally invasive surgery.
